    The Internship

    As part of our studies Geo Media & Design, we had to do an internship abroad. The internship had to last for 15 weeks and it needed to have something to do with spatial data. What better company than Sweco Norge AS to complete this adventure? This Portfolio gives an impression of the projects we have worked on during the 15 week internship so far and what projects are yet to come...

    Story Maps

    Using Story Maps is a new and exciting way of visualizing and showcasing data. By combining the power of maps and other multi media with narrative text, the author can tell a story and bring the reader along on a journey. Perfect for presenting a project!
